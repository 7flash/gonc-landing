Hey Product Hunt! 👋

If you’ve ever had 15 tabs open in your IDE trying to balance a complex mental model in your head, you know the exact pain that led me to build **GitMaps**.

As developers, we’ve been restricted to interacting with code through a 1-dimensional vertical scroll and endless browser-style tabs. Our codebases are complex, multi-dimensional networks, but our tools flatten them. 

GitMaps moves your development experience to a 2D spatial canvas. 

Here's how it changes the workflow:
- 🗺️ **Bird's-Eye View:** You can zoom out to instantly grasp the architecture, then zoom in seamlessly to drill into the detailed function logic.
- 🔗 **Visual Tracing:** Lines physically connect your modules and dependencies, giving an explicit, unmistakable view of how pieces fit together.
- 🧠 **Spatial Memory:** Humans remember *where* things are far better than *what* they are named. With an infinite canvas, you start building a geographic, muscle memory of your codebase.

We've got an interactive demo right on the landing page where you can drag code nodes around yourself. 

I'd love to hear your thoughts on spatial development and toolings. Drop any questions or feedback below, I'll be hanging out in the comments all day! 🚀
